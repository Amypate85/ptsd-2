We are Jim and Lily and living the simple life saved our life

Jim served in the navy for two years during World War II and Lily served as a navy nurse during the Korean and Vietnam wars. We are Veterans ourselves and this is our home. It's a wellness center and retreat. We ar looking for veterans who can help with things such as horse training and care, and then eventually teaching various classes.

We are still in the early stages preparing the ranch so that veterans can come and ride horses, relax, take classes, meditate, etc.Not only help the individual [with PTSD], but treat the whole family. They all are affected by what happens to one individual
